WHO WE ARE

Established in June 2021 by Dr Augustine Maruva[enyika] Tirivangana, SAMPET FAMILY TRUST is a family-based Trust built on the fundamentals of breaking the cycle of poverty, alleviating the financial troubles of the immediate family born of Sam and Pet [the canonized Patriarch and Matriarch through the bloodline of their 8th Son, Augustine, hereby the Settlor] but also stretching to the larger community of the under-privileged, giving them a chance to live a dignified life and measure of happiness; inspired by the tenets of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR), The African Charter on the Rights of the African Child and the founding values of the Zimbabwean Consti...tution. Take note that the declaration and these charters may not be legally binding but carry moral weight because they have been adopted by the international community.

The Universal Declaration of Human Rights begins by recognizing that "the inherent dignity of all members of the human family is the foundation of freedom, justice and peace in the world". It declares that human rights are universal-to be enjoyed by all people, no matter who they are or where they live. It also includes economic, social and cultural rights, such as the right to social security, health and education.

Articles 1, 2 and 26 of the UDHR are particularly instructive to the founding principles of SAMPET FAMILY TRUST. More About Us

Education

The “international” outlook of SAMPET INSTITUTIONS derives from the universal nature of its overall scope. The syllabuses addressed are drawn from a wide range of institutions in Zimbabwe, the SADC, and Africa and beyond. The online mode of delivery, by its nature, defies passport boundaries. Informed by the philosophical concept of “the sovereign individual”, the online methodology targets every global citizen, newly born, infant, adolescent, grown-up and ‘grown-out’ alike. This approach complements Klaus Schwab’s craving, which is shared by every global citizen: to ‘shape the future of the fourth industrial revolution’ and ensure that mankind benefits optimally from the digital capabilities and affordances of ICT.

Health And Wellness

Wellness is a modern word with ancient roots. The key tenets of wellness as both preventive and holistic can be traced back to ancient civilizations from the East (India, China) to the West (Greece, Rome). In 19th-century Europe and the United States, a variety of intellectual, religious and medical movements developed in parallel with conventional medicine. In the Health Sector in Zimbabwe, we assist under privileged Cancer patients. This is done through providing them with accessories needed for their treatment. Cancer Treatment is very expensive and we have sought to provide relief to those affected with more emphasis on patients whose income is below the poverty datum line.

Community Empowerment

Community empowerment refers to the process of enabling communities to increase control over their lives. ... Community empowerment, therefore, is more than the involvement, participation or engagement of communities. It implies community ownership and action that explicitly aims at social and political change. Our Foundation enters into partnerships with people in marginalised communities who have viable project proposals. At present most of our projects involve farming,education and media. The thrust of these partnerships is to empower the local communities and reduce dependency.

Donations

We assist the less privileged communities through distribution of both second hand and brand new basic goods that are essential in their day to day lives.
This includes clothes, sanitary wear, food stuffs, medical drugs and accessories among other items.
Our target groups are orphanages, old people's homes, psychiatric homes and people living in drought prone remote areas in Zimbabwe.
